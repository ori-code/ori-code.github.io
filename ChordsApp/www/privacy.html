Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

Welcome to Chords App by The Faith Sound. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Chords App, we may collect:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • User Content: Songs you create, save, or upload to our service.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including features accessed and session data.
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Chords App service
  • Save and sync your songs across devices
  • Enable live session features for worship teams
  • Manage your subscription and account
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Communicate important updates about the service

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in operating our app (e.g., Firebase, Stripe for payments)

5. Live Sessions

When you create or join a live session, other participants in that session can see the song content being shared. Session codes are temporary and sessions can be ended at any time by the leader.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your songs
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage to save your preferences (like theme settings) and maintain your session. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless necessary for functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Chords App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

Email: thefaithsound@gmail.com
